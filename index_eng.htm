Introduction

Who am I ?

My name is Yannis M'RAD.
I am currently working at CGI Québec as a programmer analyst.
My job consists in developing components for Web / Cloud apps (.NET and Azure), and supporting them.
Besides, I like developing video games, in order to keep my skills up to date, challenge myself and have fun.
I have a Master's Degree in Computer Graphics (University of Limoges, France) and a Master's degree in Computer Science (University of Québec at Chicoutimi, Canada).
My studies are hence oriented towards realtime rendering / 3D, and video games development is the continuity of this.

Thanks to my past experiences, I have acquired skills in various domains such as web, mobile and virtual reality, which is a big plus that helps me being polyvalent and able to understand the specific issues related to these different fields.
Game programming, and interactive applications in general, remains a domain I am highly interested in, that is why occasionally I'm working on games (during game jams for example).
You can check in the Sample Work section a few examples of the projects I have worked on (video games and VR applications).
